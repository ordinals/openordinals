◉ Open Ordinals

The Open Ordinals Institute, a California nonprofit corporation in the process of applying for 501(c)(3) status, is a public charity dedicated to nurturing and upholding a thriving ecosystem for the free and open-source development of the Bitcoin Ordinals protocol. We are staunch believers in the essential role of free and open-source software for the future of the internet and the world.

The Open Ordinals Institute supports its mission through our contributor program by supporting open source developers in efforts that align with the organization's goals.

The board of directors reviews contributor proposals thoroughly to ensure donations support the Institute’s core activities:

  • Mentorship opportunities for contributors to open source Bitcoin ordinals development
  • Research and education enabling less technically proficient users to access the ordinals ecosystem
  • Ongoing support and maintenance of the ordinals reference client
  • Development and enhancement of the ordinals protocols
  • Empowering underserved individuals with the tools and knowledge to access the bitcoin and ordinals ecosystem


The board considers a contributor's application under the following criteria:

  • Capabilities and expertise
  • Alignment with the organization's mission
  • Attention to detail
  • Creative problem-solving skills
  • Mentoring experience


Contributors must receive a majority approval by the board, and are expected to provide regular progress updates to the board.

Interested in becoming an Open Ordinal Institute contributor? Submit your proposal to info@ordinals.org outlining your proposed work, your experience, and how your skills align with the mission and core activities of the Open Ordinals Institute. Please provide your name and email address so we can contact you regarding your proposal.

There are numerous ways you can assist in achieving this mission. You can:

  • Donate to the Open Ordinals Institute
  • Contribute to the Ordinals protocol
  • Spread the word about the Open Ordinals initiative

Every little support counts. We appreciate your commitment to our cause. Thank you!
